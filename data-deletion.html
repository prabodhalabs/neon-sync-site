Data Deletion

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Neon Sync is designed with privacy in mind. We do not maintain user accounts or store personal information on our servers. Below is how you can delete any data associated with your use of the App.

1. Local Game Data

All game progress, settings, hint counts, and undo counts are stored locally on your device. To delete this data completely, simply uninstall the App from your device. No data will remain after uninstallation.

2. Advertising Data (Google AdMob)

Google AdMob may associate an advertising identifier with your device to serve relevant ads. You can reset or delete this identifier at any time:

  • Android: Go to Settings → Privacy → Ads and tap "Reset advertising ID" or "Delete advertising ID".

For more details, see Google's guide on advertising ID.

3. Analytics Data (Firebase Analytics)

Firebase Analytics collects anonymized usage data (such as game events and device information) using a Firebase Installation ID. This data cannot be tied back to you personally. To reset it, uninstall and reinstall the App — this generates a new anonymous identifier.

4. Purchase Records (RevenueCat)

If you have made in-app purchases, RevenueCat stores an anonymous record of your purchase and entitlement status. To request deletion of this data, please email us at:

prabodhalabs@gmail.com

Please include "Data Deletion Request" in the subject line. We will process your request within 30 days.
